Privacy Policy – Habits + Karma

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used when you use the Karma Tracker application.

We may collect certain data automatically to help us understand how the app is used and to improve its functionality. This data may include:

We do not collect personal information such as your name, physical address, or contact details.

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

The app may use third-party services (such as analytics and attribution SDKs) that collect data for tracking purposes, including linking app activity with device identifiers for advertising and analytics.

4. Permissions

5. Data Sharing

Collected data may be shared with trusted third-party service providers solely for analytics, advertising attribution, and app performance monitoring, in accordance with applicable laws.
